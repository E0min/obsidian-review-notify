### 주요업무

[About the Role]
At TwelveLabs, our foundation models — Marengo and Pegasus — are redefining what's possible with video understanding. But models alone don't create value. Products do.

As an Engineering Manager, Products, you will own the bridge between our model capabilities and the experiences our customers actually use. You'll lead the Korea-based product engineering team end-to-end: setting the technical direction, shipping production-grade software, and building a team that moves with both speed and craft.

This isn't a role where you manage from a distance. You'll stay close to the code — driving architecture decisions, unblocking your team on hard technical problems, and holding a high bar for what good looks like. At the same time, you'll operate as a true owner of the product engineering surface: translating ambiguous goals into clear plans, partnering with Product and Science teams, and ensuring that every new model capability finds its way into the hands of customers quickly and correctly.

We're at a stage where the decisions you make — about the team, the architecture, and the product — will have lasting impact. If you're energized by building things that don't yet exist, leading people who care deeply about their craft, and operating at the intersection of AI and product, this is the role for you.


[In this role, you will]
Turning AI Models into Products
• Own the end-to-end product engineering surface built on top of TwelveLabs' foundation models — Marengo and Pegasus. Translate model capabilities and constraints into reliable, high-quality customer-facing features and workflows.
• Drive the product engineering roadmap for the Korea team: from understanding customer needs and use cases to shipping well-crafted, production-grade software.
• Work closely with Science and ML teams to stay ahead of model developments, and ensure new capabilities are productionized quickly and correctly.
• Hold a high bar for product quality — what customers see, touch, and feel matters as much as what's under the hood.

Build and lead the Engineering Team
• Lead, grow, and manage a fullstack product engineering team in Korea. Own the full management lifecycle: recruiting, onboarding, 1:1s, performance reviews, and career development.
• Build a culture of ownership, speed, and craft — where the team ships with urgency without sacrificing quality or user experience.

Technical Leadership & Execution
• Lead architectural and technical decision-making for the team. Stay hands-on where it matters: system design reviews, critical-path decisions, and high-risk technical areas.
• Translate product and company goals into clear technical plans, milestones, and timelines. Make trade-offs explicit and manage dependencies across pods.
• Drive fast, predictable release cycles through structured planning, weekly reviews, and retrospectives that lead to continuous improvement. Own delivery, quality, and operational excellence end-to-end.

Partner Across the Organization
• Collaborate closely with Product, GTM, and Science teams to align on roadmap, priorities, and customer needs.
